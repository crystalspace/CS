Crystal Space detailed change log.
http://www.crystalspace3d.org/

This used to be the history entries for Crystal Space. To lessen the
maintenance burden this has now been fully migrated into subversion. There
are a number of ways to access recent log information:

1. svn log
	Tips: Use svn log --limit 10 to get the 10 latest changes or 
	svn log -r {2008-04-20} to get them from a specific day until today.

2. trac timeline at http://crystalspace3d.org/trac/CS/timeline

Please see "history.old" for less recent historical entries.
